ABOUT US

Founded in 2021, MM Jewellers is a small, independent jewellery label based in Faisalabad, Pakistan. Our range of delicate sterling silver and gemstone adornments are designed to become a much loved extension of you. Our collections are inspired by spirituality and the modern bohemian with influences from traditional Balinese and Thai silversmithing. Crafted in hypoallergenic sterling silver, our jewels are safe for those with sensitive skin and ideal for everyday wear. Driven by quality and affordability, we ultimately aspire to make you feel beautiful and empowered in your Jewellery. Lowering our impact, and preserving our earth is of the utmost importance to us. Our jewels are ethically produced and are delivered in eco-conscious packaging. As we evolve, we continue to find new ways to operate in a sustainable manner. As a 1% for the Planet member we have committed to donating 1% of our annual revenue, towards environmental causes to promote a more sustainable future.
